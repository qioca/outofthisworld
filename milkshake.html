Out Of This World: Intro

March 18, 2021 by Ben James

Hi. I'm Ben, the owner of Out Of This World. I have a small collection of top-level domain names that are available for acquisition. Please feel free to contact me with an interest on a TLD you see showcased on my site.

My involvement in the Handshake domain ecosystem stems from a background in traditional domain investing and an optimistic belief that there is a strong future in self-custodial TLD adoption in the blockchain-based Web3 internet.

I invest in Handshake for lots of top-level reasons. Just as the medium is the message, a domain transports and tells its own experience.

A Handshake top-level domain naturally comes with permanent stewardship, but personally I feel it a privilege. A decentralized internet needs responsible development to lay its foundation with HNS as its cornerstone. What better block to use for it than a TLD? Nothing makes me giddier than seeing folks build on a superb brand based on it's exact-match domain name, because it tells me they've put thought into the PRESENTATION so the DESTINATION better be amazing.
